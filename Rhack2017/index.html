    Saaksharta: Ek Nayi Umang

    As per BJP's manifesto 'Suraj Sankalp Patra' 2013, Rajasthan's progress has been declining with the lowest female literacy rates, huge unemployment and dearth of infrastructure and workforce for runing schools and colleges.

    We at Nayi Umang strive to create digitized classrooms for village dwellers with video lectures incorporating acedemics, ways to enhance current livelihood conditions as well as lessons on social and moral ethics for children as well as adults. With separate lectures for men, women and children by the many unemployed professionals in Rajasthan, we strive to enable a major part of the state to earn a livelihood.
